A Second floor of cages

You crest the top of the stair you find your self in a much smaller room filled with cages stacked high and close, with a bit of exporation you conclude that the small room was not so small but a maze of cages leading nowhere, just a storage room. However as you come to a end of your exploration you hear very low tone grunting a chuffing and the sound of metal clanking and tinging as something moved up the stairs.
Option1: Hide from what come in the shadows waiting for a chance to run.
Option2: Grab a poll or bar from an unassembiled cage and wait right at the entrence to wrap the poll over the head of whatever comes through.

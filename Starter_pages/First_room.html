A Cell Room

You awaken, slowly searching your surroundings, you are in a cell or large cage of some kind, it is dark but small hints of light in the room allow for a modicume of sight, the room is empty and is so quiet you feel as if your breath is giveing your posision away to anything in the room, with a quick examination of the cage it is locked with a extremly rusted and bent bolt lock.
Option1: Kick the cage at the lock in an attempt to bust the locked cage's door open.
Option2: Wait in the cage for something to happen to hear something or see something.
Option3: Take a moment to unbolt the lock for the cage by putting your fingers throught the gaps and pushing the bolt open.
